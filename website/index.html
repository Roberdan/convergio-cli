Frequently Asked Questions

Is Convergio really free?

Yes! Convergio is open source and free to use. You can use local models at no cost, or connect to cloud providers (Claude, OpenAI, etc.) with your own API keys.

Does it work offline?

Absolutely. With local models (MLX or Apple Foundation Models), Convergio works completely offline. Your data never leaves your Mac.

What about privacy?

Privacy is core to Convergio. Local processing means your conversations, code, and data stay on your device. No telemetry, no data collection.

Can I use it for commercial projects?

Yes! Convergio is licensed under MIT. Use it for personal projects, startups, or enterprise applications.

How do I switch between local and cloud models?

Use the -l flag for local models or configure your preferred provider in the setup wizard. Convergio automatically selects the best option based on the task.

What makes Apple Foundation Models special?

Apple's 3B on-device model integrates natively with macOS 26+, offering structured output, tool calling, and seamless Siri integration - all with zero latency and complete privacy.
